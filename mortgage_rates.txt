## Sweden Mortgage Rates 2025

Sweden's mortgage rates declined throughout 2025 as the Riksbank lowered its policy rate. The most recent data shows:

The rates varied by loan structure. Fixed-rate mortgages with 1-5 year initial terms averaged **2.88% in September 2025**, while floating-rate mortgages averaged **2.80%**[5]. Approximately 73% of outstanding housing loans used floating interest rates[5].

Looking ahead, mortgage rates in Sweden are projected to remain around **2.80% through 2026** and rise to approximately **3.05% in 2027**[2]. The Riksbank maintained its policy rate at **1.75%** as of December 2025[4][6].
